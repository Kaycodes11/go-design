CSS Units

Absolute(fixed) units

  • cm - centimeters
  • mm - millimeters
  • in - inches
  • px - pixels
  • pt - points
  • pc - picas

Relative (fluid) Units

  • em - Relation to the container
  • ex - Relation to X height of current font
  • ch - Relation to the width of 0
  • rem - Relation to font size of root element
  • vw - Relation to 1% of viewport width
  • vh - Relation to 1% of viewport height
  • vmin - Relation to 1% of viewport smaller dimension
  • vmax - Relation to 1% of viewport larger dimension
  • % - Relation to parent element
